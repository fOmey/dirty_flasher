dirty_flasher

User friendly web flashing tool used to update section80 devices:

  • Game Over
  • End Game
  • T-Display

This will flash the latest update to your device. To get started, connect your device to your computer and click the CONNECT button.

Game Over & End Game must be in DOWNLOAD mode to flash, you can enter it by:

  1. Holding down the BOOT button.
  2. While the BOOT button is held, press REBOOT button.
  3. Release the BOOT button.

Game Over:

The flasher is not available because your browser does not support Web Serial. Open this page in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge instead (but not on your iOS device).

End Game:

The flasher is not available because your browser does not support Web Serial. Open this page in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge instead (but not on your iOS device).

T-Display:

The flasher is not available because your browser does not support Web Serial. Open this page in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge instead (but not on your iOS device).
