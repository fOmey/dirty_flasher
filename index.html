dirty_flasher

User friendly web flashing tool used to update section80 devices:

  • Game Over
  • End Game
  • T-Display

This will flash your device to the latest update. To get started, connect your device to your computer and hit one of the CONNECT buttons.

You must be in DOWNLOAD mode to flash, you can enter it by:

  1. Holding down the BOOT button.
  2. While the BOOT button is held, press REBOOT button.
  3. Release the BOOT button.
